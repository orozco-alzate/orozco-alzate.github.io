Birdwatching

Bird watching is my favourite hobby. I practice this outdoor activity with a group of friends of the birds of Caldas (Colombia) as well as with the group of birdwatchers from the Sociedad Caldense de Ornitología - SCO. Bird watching is not just an aesthetic pleasure but also a good physical exercise since implies a great deal of walking through forest paths. In addition, cognitive skills are used for identifying bird species, remembering scientific names and recognizing different bird sounds.
